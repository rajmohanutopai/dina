# Dina Interaction Areas

99 conceptual areas where users interact with Dina. These represent the full surface of how a sovereign personal AI serves daily life — from memory and health to agent delegation and trust networks.

This is not a feature list. It is the problem space. Each area may involve multiple Dina capabilities (vault storage, reminders, D2D messaging, agent delegation, trust network, approval gating, MCP tools).

---

## Memory & Identity

**1. Personal Memory & Preferences**
Remembering likes, dislikes, sizes, habits, and recalling them in context later.

**2. Knowledge Ingestion**
Storing articles, classifying notes into correct personas, importing external data.

**3. Journaling & Reflection**
Daily captures, weekly summaries, theme extraction over time.

**4. Photography & Memories**
Session bookings, trip highlights, family milestones.

**5. Security & Identity Management**
DID status, paired devices, persona states, key rotation, session management.

**6. Passwords & Account Recovery**
Security questions, signup emails, recovery hints (stored in locked vault).

**7. Legacy & Estate Planning**
Will location, attorney contacts, life insurance policies, last update dates.

## Communication

**8. D2D Communication (Dina-to-Dina)**
Encrypted peer-to-peer messaging between Dina instances — arrival signals, coordination, social updates.

**9. Email Operations**
Searching inbox, drafting replies, summarizing threads, sending with approval gating.

**10. Writing Assistance**
Rewriting messages, drafting updates, tone adjustment.

**11. Notifications & Alerts**
Task completion alerts, approval timeout warnings, proactive nudges.

**12. Proactive Intelligence**
Lateness warnings, important follow-up surfacing, context-triggered nudges.

## Scheduling & Time

**15. Habit Formation**
Daily stretch reminders, journaling prompts, consistency tracking.

**16. Goal Tracking**
Fitness targets, learning milestones, monthly objectives, progress reviews.

## Agent Safety & Delegation

**17. Approvals & Permissions**
Human-in-the-loop gating for risky agent actions — email sends, data access, financial operations.

**18. Task Delegation**
Handing off research, comparison, or multi-step work to an agent that runs autonomously.

**19. Multi-Agent Orchestration**
Routing workflows through multiple agents — email + calendar + research in one delegation.

**20. Policy & Rule Management**
Setting action risk levels, configuring persona access tiers, agent permissions.

**21. Audit & Traceability**
Who approved what, why a message was sent, action history.

## Research & Analysis

These areas cut across multiple domains:

- **Search & Research** — Web lookups, article finding, current rules/regulations
- **Decision Support** — Ranking options by personal criteria, cost-benefit with vault context
- **Proactive Intelligence** — Lateness warnings, follow-up surfacing, context-triggered nudges

---

*Each area exercises different Dina capabilities. Most involve vault storage (auto-classified into health/finance/general/work). Many trigger reminders. Some require approval gating. Some use D2D messaging. Some delegate to agents. The Trust Network connects purchase and vendor areas. The goal is for Dina to be the single place where a person's entire life context lives — sovereign, encrypted, and under their control.*
